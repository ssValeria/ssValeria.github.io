Improvement and Defense of the Planet


  • With each improvement in your defense technology, your defensive structures become more robust and resistant. Strengthen your missile launchers, ion and plasma cannons, and make sure that any invader will pay a high price for trying to penetrate your defenses.

  • Become an unstoppable force in the universe! By improving your attack technology, Every ship in your fleet becomes a more efficient destruction machine. Improve your hunts light, heavy, battleships and battleships to deal more damage in each confrontation.

Fighter Construction

  • Fast, agile and lethal. Light fighters are the vanguard of your navy, perfect for quick attacks and reconnaissance missions. Produce these fighters It is crucial to maintain an active defense and surprise offenses.

  • With greater destructive capacity and endurance, heavy fighters are ideal for longer and tougher confrontations. His brute force can change the course of a battle.

Ship Construction

  • Deploy robust battleships that can withstand heavy damage and strike hard. Battleships are the giants of space, capable of absorbing significant damage and strike back with devastating force. They are the core of any self-respecting fleet.

  • Manufacture armored ships with high defense and destructive capacity. Maximum protection and great offensive power. Armored ships are perfect for players who value defense and offense in equal measure. They are difficult to destroy and dangerous in any confrontation.

Advanced Defense

  • Missile launchers are your first line of defense, providing an initial barrier against any invasion. They are quick to build and effective in large numbers.

  • Ion cannons offer precise and powerful defense, capable of neutralizing threats before they can that reach your critical infrastructures. They are the next step in the evolution of your defenses.

  • The maximum expression of planetary defense. Plasma cannons are destructive and can disintegrate enemy ships with a single shot. Its mere presence deters potential attackers.

Planet Management

  • The key to a prosperous empire is efficient resource management. Produce and store metal and deuterium, and use it wisely to build and upgrade your fleet and defenses.

  • Keep an eye on all your planet's key stats, from resource production to the state of your defense and fleet. Information is power, and this feature allows you to always be one step ahead.

Battle Simulation

  • Show your might in epic space battles! Send your fleet against other planets and compete for galactic domination. Every battle is an opportunity to show your strategy and command skills.

  • After each battle, review the results in detail. Learn from your victories and defeats to improve your tactics and better prepare for future confrontations.
