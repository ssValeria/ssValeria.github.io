PLANET WARS

Planet Wars is an online strategy app inspired by the popular browser game Ogame. Players are immersed in a universe full of planets, resources, fleets of ships and technologies. Each player represents a planetary ruler, charged with managing its resources, developing technologies and strengthening its defenses while navigating a cosmos full of danger. In this vast universe, there are other planets whose rulers intend to conquer and plunder. Strategy, diplomacy and tactics are essential to defend our planet and expand our galactic empire.
